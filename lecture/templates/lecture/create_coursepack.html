What is FUL-VC?

FUL-VC is an app that allows you to upload and store all of your lecture materials on the cloud.

How do I add Course materials?

First, create a new coursepack by filling out the form on this page. Once an coursepack is created you will be able to add/upload course materials.

What are some thumbnail best practices?

  • Have a resolution of 512x512
  • Use common image formats such as .JPG, .GIF, or .PNG
  • Remain under the 2MB limit.
  • Square images look best
